Namhyoung Kim

Quantitative AI Researcher at Risk X tech Lab
Ph.D., Industrial Engineering · Hanyang University
✉ ai.namhyoung@gmail.com ↗ GitHub in LinkedIn ↓ CV

I am a Quantitative AI Researcher at Risk X tech Lab and a Ph.D. in Industrial Engineering from Hanyang University, advised by Prof. Jae Wook Song at the Financial Innovation & Decision Analytics (FINX) Lab.

My research lies at the intersection of machine learning and quantitative finance, focusing on representation learning for financial markets with applications to portfolio management, generative modeling, and derivative pricing. I am particularly interested in vector-quantized latent factor models, physics-informed neural operators, and language-aligned representations that bridge fundamental financial priors with modern deep architectures.

Recent work on vector-quantized discrete latent factors has been published in Knowledge-Based Systems and presented at ICAIF '25 (oral, 15.5%). Always open to research discussions — feel free to reach out.
